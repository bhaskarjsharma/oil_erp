Oil India Limited ERP Mobile App

This mobile application has been developed by Oil India Limited to provide employees, stakeholders, contractors, and external business partners with convenient and secure access to essential services and information on the fly, from anywhere.

The app offers:

  • Latest news, official circulars, annual reports and policy updates from Oil India Limited.
  • Access to a range of ERP and HR functions for employees, such as leave requests, attendance, benefits information, and internal communication.
  • Mobile-based work progress updates.
  • Publicly accessible E-Tendering Portal for vendors and business associates to explore and participate in tendering opportunities.
  • Direct access to the Vendor Invoice Portal for seamless submission, tracking, and management of vendor invoices.
  • Access to the Vendor Zone, providing business partners with personalized updates, interaction channels, and essential documents.

This app is free to download and use, with some features accessible to all users and additional services available to registered users upon login.
