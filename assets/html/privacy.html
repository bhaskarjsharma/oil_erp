Privacy Policy – Oil India Limited

We respect and protect your privacy. This mobile application, developed by Oil India Limited, is designed to operate without collecting personally identifiable information (PII) from users unless explicitly required for specific functionalities (such as secure login for registered users).

1. Data Collection

  • The app collects only minimal data required for authentication and operational purposes.
  • No personal information is collected from guest or public users.
  • We do not track your location, device ID, or any behavioral data.

2. Use of Data

  • Any data collected (for example, during login) is used solely to enable secure access to relevant services for registered users.
  • No data is shared, sold, or transferred to third parties.

3. Data Security

  • As personal data is not stored or collected by default, the risk of data misuse is minimal.
  • Any login-related data is handled securely and is not retained beyond the session.

4. User Rights

  • As no personal data is stored, there are no applicable rights related to data access, correction, or deletion.

5. Policy Updates
